VISIT BATANES

Cafe du Tukon

Most of the vegetables, beef, and pork served are locally produced and organically raised by farmers of Sitio Tuko. Some of the products all come from the backyard of Fundacion Pacita Hotel in Batanes where they also produce their own jams and breads.

Address: Sitio Tukon Chanarian, Tukon, Basco, 3900 Batanes

Phone Number: +63 998 972 0028

Korner Hauz Cafe

Discover the rustic charm of Korner Hauz Cafe in Batanes, where every sip and bite is a journey through the flavors of this breathtaking archipelago. Perched in a cozy corner, the cafe invites you to indulge in locally-inspired dishes and artisanal coffee, all while soaking in the stunning vistas that surround this tranquil haven.

Address: National Road, Brgy. Kayvaluganan, Basco, Philippines

Phone Number: +63 929 402 6494

Octagon Bed and Dine

Nestled amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Batanes, Octagon Bed and Dine invites you to savor a culinary journey like no other. Indulge in delectable local flavors served with a modern twist, while surrounded by the unique charm of our octagonal-themed restaurant. An unforgettable dining experience awaits at the heart of Batanes.

Address: National Rd, Brgy Kaychanarianan, Basco, Philippines

Phone Number: +63 960 229 2730

Pension Ivatan

Discover tranquility at Pension Ivatan in the enchanting Batanes. A cozy retreat where traditional Ivatan hospitality meets modern comfort. Immerse yourself in the warmth of our accommodations and savor the serenity of this island haven.

Address: National Road, Brgy. Kayvaluganan, Basco, Philippines

Phone Number: +63 999 881 6787
